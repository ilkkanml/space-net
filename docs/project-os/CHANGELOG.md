## Milestone 10 Hotfix — City Builder Camera Controls

- Camera controls were aligned with common city-builder / Cities-style camera expectations.
- Mouse wheel now only zooms in/out and no longer changes camera tilt automatically.
- Q/E smooth 360-degree camera rotation remains active.
- Middle mouse hold + left/right drag rotates the camera.
- Middle mouse hold + up/down drag adjusts camera pitch/tilt.
- Build mode, remove mode, conveyor drag placement, remove drag toggle and save/load behavior are preserved.
