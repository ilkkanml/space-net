# S.P.A.C.E. NET — Atmosphere Preservation Protocol

## Core Preservation Rule

S.P.A.C.E. NET's strength is not only what it adds.
It is what it refuses to add.

## Must Preserve

- Silence-first philosophy.
- Rare but meaningful E.V.A. presence.
- Calm progression pacing.
- Industrial loneliness continuity.
- Observe-and-maintain gameplay feeling.
- Low-noise UI.
- Short and restrained E.V.A. lines.
- Distant industrial ambience.
- Mission pacing that does not rush the player.
- Conveyor observation comfort.

## Dangerous Future Directions

Avoid:
- Objective treadmill.
- Mobile-management dopamine loops.
- Constant success feedback.
- Loud sci-fi spectacle.
- Permanent HUD pressure.
- Notification spam.
- Companion AI feeling.
- Over-guidance.
- Excessive tutorialization.
- Neon overload.
- Constant music presence.

## E.V.A. Restraint

E.V.A. is not a chat companion.
E.V.A. is not a tutorial window.
E.V.A. is the distant voice of a damaged network.

Preferred feeling:
- calm
- controlled
- rare
- slightly mysterious
- system-like
- emotionally restrained

## Tone Keywords

Use carefully:
- signal
- flow
- stability
- network
- silence
- restored
- pattern
- sector

Avoid overusing:
- operator
- mission completed
- task successful
- warning detected
- system online

## Final Identity

v0.2 should feel like:

A silent industrial network slowly breathing again.
